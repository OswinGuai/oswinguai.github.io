This book provides a systematic treatment of formal methods for industrial data analytics based on the CRISP-DM (Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining) framework. It addresses the gap between ad-hoc data analysis practices and rigorous engineering methodologies, offering structured approaches for each phase of the industrial data analysis lifecycle — from business understanding through data preparation, modeling, evaluation, and deployment.
