This paper presents a structured framework for analyzing and specifying interpretable requirements of machine learning applications. It addresses the critical challenge of ensuring that ML systems meet explainability requirements from stakeholders, providing systematic guidance for capturing, organizing, and validating interpretability needs throughout the ML development lifecycle.
