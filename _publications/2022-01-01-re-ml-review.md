This paper provides a comprehensive review and reflection on requirements engineering (RE) practices for machine learning applications. It identifies the unique challenges that ML poses to traditional RE — including data-dependent behavior, non-deterministic outputs, and evolving performance — and proposes structured methodologies for capturing, specifying, and validating ML system requirements throughout the development lifecycle.
