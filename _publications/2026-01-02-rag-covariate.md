This work introduces a novel paradigm for time series analysis by combining retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) with covariate time series data. By retrieving relevant historical patterns and augmenting generation models with covariate context, this approach opens new avenues for time series reasoning and forecasting. *Equal contribution.
