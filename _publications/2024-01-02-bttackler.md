BTTackler introduces a paradigm shift in deep learning hyperparameter optimization. Instead of blindly searching the hyperparameter space, it first diagnoses the root causes of training failures (e.g., gradient vanishing, overfitting, underfitting) and then guides optimization to targeted regions, reducing the search space by orders of magnitude and significantly improving development efficiency for industrial AI applications.
