Aura addresses a fundamental challenge in industrial time series forecasting: how to effectively integrate multi-dimensional exogenous variables (weather, operational schedules, market conditions) into predictive models. The framework provides a universal mechanism for exogenous information integration that significantly improves forecasting accuracy in aviation and energy domains.
