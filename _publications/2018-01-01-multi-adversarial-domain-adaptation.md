Multi-Adversarial Domain Adaptation enables knowledge transfer from multiple source domains simultaneously, addressing the common industrial scenario where data from several similar but distinct environments must be combined to improve target-domain performance. By employing multiple adversarial discriminators, the framework aligns feature distributions across domains while preserving domain-specific characteristics, achieving superior transfer performance compared to single-source approaches.
