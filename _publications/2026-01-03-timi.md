TiMi extends time series foundation models with a multimodal mixture-of-experts architecture that integrates heterogeneous industrial signals — numeric sensor data, text logs, and image inputs — for unified time series modeling. This enables comprehensive understanding of complex industrial processes that generate multi-modal data streams.
