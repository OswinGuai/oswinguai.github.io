This paper provides a comprehensive framework for detecting out-of-distribution inputs in prognostics and health management systems for renewable energy. By identifying anomalous conditions that deviate from known operational patterns, the framework enables early warning and graceful degradation, ensuring trustworthy AI operation in safety-critical renewable energy applications.
