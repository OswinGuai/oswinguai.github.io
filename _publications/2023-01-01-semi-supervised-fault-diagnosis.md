Industrial fault data is scarce and expensive to collect. This paper leverages semi-supervised adversarial discriminative learning to achieve high-accuracy fault diagnosis for wind turbines with minimal labeled data. The approach combines adversarial training with discriminative feature learning, enabling effective knowledge transfer from abundant unlabeled operational data to the critical fault classification task.
