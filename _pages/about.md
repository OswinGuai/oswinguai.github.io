I am an Assistant Researcher at the [School of Software, Tsinghua University](https://www.thss.tsinghua.edu.cn). My research sits at the intersection of **AI Engineering** and **Industrial Intelligence**, with a mission to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence advances and real-world industrial deployment.

<hr class="section-divider"/>

<div class="vision-box">
How can we systematically engineer AI technologies to deliver reliable, scalable, and generalizable value in complex industrial scenarios?
</div>

<hr class="section-divider"/>

## Research Directions

<div class="research-card ts-intelligence">
<div class="card-title"><span class="card-icon">📊</span> Industrial Time Series Intelligence — From Data to Decisions</div>
<div class="card-subtitle">Foundation models, multimodal modeling, exogenous integration, domain-adaptive transfer</div>
<div class="card-content">
Building foundation models and specialized algorithms for time series understanding, forecasting, and anomaly detection in industrial settings. This encompasses multimodal time series modeling, exogenous variable integration, domain-adaptive transfer learning, and retrieval-augmented generation for time series analysis.
</div>
</div>

<div class="research-card ai-engineering">
<div class="card-title"><span class="card-icon">🔧</span> AI Engineering — From Models to Systems</div>
<div class="card-subtitle">Requirements engineering, hyperparameter optimization, agile development for ML</div>
<div class="card-content">
Developing systematic methodologies for requirements engineering, development orchestration, and quality assurance of AI-powered software. This includes interpretable requirements analysis for ML applications, efficient hyperparameter optimization frameworks, and agile development paradigms tailored for industrial AI software.
</div>
</div>

<div class="research-card generalizable-ai">
<div class="card-title"><span class="card-icon">🌐</span> Generalizable Industrial AI — From Lab to Field</div>
<div class="card-subtitle">OOD detection, fault diagnosis, domain adaptation for cross-environment transfer</div>
<div class="card-content">
Ensuring AI systems generalize reliably across diverse real-world industrial environments. This covers out-of-distribution detection for prognostics and health management, semi-supervised fault diagnosis for complex equipment, and domain adaptation techniques that enable knowledge transfer across industrial environments.
</div>
</div>
