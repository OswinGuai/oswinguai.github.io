I am an **Assistant Researcher** at the [School of Software, Tsinghua University](https://www.thss.tsinghua.edu.cn). My research sits at the intersection of **AI Engineering** and **Industrial Intelligence**, with a mission to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence advances and real-world industrial deployment.

<div class="vision-box">
How can we systematically engineer AI technologies to deliver reliable, scalable, and generalizable value in complex industrial scenarios?
</div>
