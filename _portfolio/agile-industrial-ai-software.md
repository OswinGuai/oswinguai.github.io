As the project lead of this National Key R&D Program Young Scientist project, I am developing agile development methodologies specifically tailored for industrial intelligent software. The project addresses the fundamental challenge that traditional software development processes are inadequate for AI-powered systems, which exhibit data-dependent behavior, non-deterministic outputs, and evolving performance. Our approach integrates requirements engineering for ML, efficient hyperparameter optimization (BTTackler), and continuous quality assurance into a coherent agile framework that enables rapid iteration while maintaining reliability in manufacturing environments.
