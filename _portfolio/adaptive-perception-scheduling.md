This project, part of the Sci-Tech Innovation 2030 "New Generation AI" Major Program, focuses on adaptive perception and intelligent scheduling methods for manufacturing tasks. The research combines AI-based perception systems that can adapt to varying manufacturing conditions with intelligent scheduling algorithms that optimize resource allocation in real-time. As a core technical member, I contributed to the development of domain adaptation techniques for cross-environment perception and the integration of time series analysis for predictive scheduling.
